Step into the captivating world of Tasca de España,

What was once a Spanish restaurant, a small bakery, and a small store selling daily utility items slowly transformed into a vibrant Indian-Spanish restaurant that tantalizes taste buds and celebrates the fusion of two rich culinary traditions. Tasca de España is more than just a restaurant; it is an exquisite journey that combines the vibrant flavors of India and the vibrant soul of Spain. From our delectable Indian and Spanish dishes to our irresistible desserts, bakery, and full liquor bar that serves handcrafted cocktails and fine wines, Tasca de España offers an enchanting experience that caters to families, couples seeking romance, and anyone with a discerning palate.

Mumbai Meri Jaan

At Tasca de España, we take immense pride in offering a diverse menu that showcases the rich tapestry of Indian and Spanish flavors. Our Indian dishes are a celebration of the aromatic spices, bold flavors, and vibrant colors that are synonymous with Indian cuisine. From the fiery allure of Chicken Tikka to the fragrant decadence of Lamb Biryani, each dish is meticulously crafted to transport your taste buds to the bustling streets of India. Complementing our Indian offerings, our Spanish dishes pay homage to the country's culinary traditions, with delightful creations such as Paella and Vaca frita that evoke the warmth and zest of Spain.
