Tasca de España:

Where Indian and Spanish Flavors Unite in Miami's Culinary Haven

Step into the captivating world of Tasca, a culinary gem nestled in the heart of Miami. What was once a Spanish restaurant, a small bakery, and a small store selling daily utility items slowly transformed into a vibrant Indian-Spanish restaurant that tantalizes taste buds and celebrates the fusion of two rich culinary traditions. Tasca is more than just a restaurant; it is an exquisite journey that combines the vibrant flavors of India and the vibrant soul of Spain. From our delectable Indian and Spanish dishes to our irresistible desserts, bakery, and full liquor bar that serves handcrafted cocktails and fine wines, Tasca offers an enchanting experience that caters to families, couples seeking romance, and anyone with a discerning palate.

A Transformation from Spanish restaurant to Culinary Haven

Tasca's story begins with humble origins as an Indian-Spanish restaurant and a small bakery, radiating the aroma of freshly foods that enticed passersby. However, my vision was to create a dining establishment that would be a haven for those seeking the vibrant flavors of India and the vibrant soul of Spain. With passion and dedication, I embarked on a culinary journey, transforming the Spanish restaurant into Tasca an Indian-Spanish fusion restaurant that showcases the best of both worlds. we have expanded our offerings to include a diverse range of various dishes.

Mumbai Meri Jaan

At Tasca, we take immense pride in offering a diverse menu that showcases the rich tapestry of Indian and Spanish flavors. Our Indian dishes are a celebration of the aromatic spices, bold flavors, and vibrant colors that are synonymous with Indian cuisine. From the fiery allure of Chicken Tikka to the fragrant decadence of Lamb Biryani, each dish is meticulously crafted to transport your taste buds to the bustling streets of India. Complementing our Indian offerings, our Spanish dishes pay homage to the country's culinary traditions, with delightful creations such as Paella and Vaca frita that evoke the warmth and zest of Spain.

A Sweet Symphony

Our Irresistible Desserts & Bakery

No culinary adventure is complete without indulging in the sweet pleasures of life. Tasca de España is proud to present a delectable selection of desserts that will enchant even the most discerning palates. Our in-house bakery brings forth a delightful array of treats, from traditional Indian sweets like Gulab Jamun and Ras Malai to Spanish delights like Tasca soufflé and Turron almendra. Each bite is a harmonious blend of flavors and textures, leaving you with a sense of pure bliss and a desire for more. Our bakery continues to craft fresh cheese bread and Indian bread, ensuring that every visit to Tasca de España is a delightful experience for all the senses.

A Full Liquor Bar

Handcrafted Cocktails and Fine Wines

To complement our culinary creations, Tasca proudly offers a full liquor bar that serves an array of handcrafted cocktails and a curated selection of fine wines. Our skilled mixologists have crafted a menu of signature cocktails that highlight the vibrant flavors. our bar is a haven for those seeking unique and tantalizing libations. Additionally, our extensive wine list features carefully selected wines that pair perfectly with our dishes, elevating your dining experience to new heights. Whether you prefer a classic cocktail, a glass of fine wine, or a daring fusion creation, our bar is the perfect place to unwind and savor the artistry of mixology.

Flamenco Night

The Soulful Rhythms of Tasca de España

Weekends at Tasca are a celebration of music and dance. Professional singers enchant our guests with soulful melodies, creating an atmosphere that is perfect for romantic evenings or family gatherings. Additionally, our talented dancers showcase the beauty and grace of Flamenco, captivating audiences with their intricate footwork and passionate performances. And on special occasions, we transport guests to the vibrant streets of India with mesmerizing Indian dance performances. The combination of live music and dance adds an extra touch of magic and ensures that every visit to Tasca is an unforgettable experience.

Creating Memorable Moments

A Haven for Family and Romance

At Tasca, we understand the importance of creating memorable moments. Our warm and inviting ambiance, along with the impeccable service, sets the stage for intimate dinners, family celebrations, and romantic evenings. Whether its a quiet dinner for two or a joyous gathering with loved ones, Tasca provides the perfect setting to savor delicious food, create lasting memories, and cherish the company of those you hold dear.
